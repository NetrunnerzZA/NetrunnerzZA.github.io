## Overview

We focus on practical, defensive cybersecurity for businesses. Our services help secure your network and systems through firewall configuration, VLAN segmentation, and endpoint protection. Combined with comprehensive patch management, we reduce risk and help maintain business continuity.

### Key Services

- Firewall setup and management for secure network perimeter
- VLAN design and segmentation to control internal traffic and reduce risks
- Endpoint protection deployment and management
- Identity and access management (RBAC, least privilege)
- Patch management including deployment and reporting for operating systems and applications

> Defensive cybersecurity and timely system updates are essential to keep your business safe and running smoothly.
